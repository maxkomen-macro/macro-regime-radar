Methodology
  • Data sources: Macro series sourced from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) API — CPIAUCSL, UNRATE, GS10, GS2, INDPRO, VIXCLS — updated monthly. Market data (SPY, QQQ, TLT, HYG, LQD, GLD, UUP, USO, IWM) from Polygon.io (daily OHLCV).
  • Regime classification: Growth trend derived from INDPRO rolling OLS slope; inflation trend from CPI rolling OLS slope. The four regimes (Goldilocks, Overheating, Stagflation, Recession Risk) are mapped from the sign combination of these two trends. Confidence reflects the magnitude of the z-scores.
  • Signal alerts: Five threshold-based binary signals — yield curve inversion (10Y–2Y < 0%), unemployment spike (3M change ≥ 0.3pp), CPI Hot (> 4% YoY), CPI Cold (< 1% YoY), VIX spike (> 30). Severity and duration measure how far the current reading is from the threshold and for how many consecutive months it has been active.
